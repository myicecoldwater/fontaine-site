What we do

Fontaine Agencies researches, compiles, and verifies business contact datasets that help marketing and web design agencies find qualified prospects in specific niches and cities.

We focus on public, business-facing data only – no consumer data, no financial data, and no sensitive information.

How it works

  • We identify a niche and location (for example, roofing companies in a major US city).
  • We collect business information from publicly available sources such as online business listings and maps.
  • We verify and classify each entry (for example, whether the business has a website, uses a free site, or has no site).
  • We provide the cleaned dataset to agencies in CSV format for use in their own outbound campaigns.

Data sources and compliance

All data is collected from publicly available online sources such as business directories and map listings. We do not sell or handle any consumer data, financial data, or sensitive personal information.

Our role is to compile, verify, and structure publicly available business information into useful datasets for agencies that already operate in these niches.
